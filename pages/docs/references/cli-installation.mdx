<h1>CLI Installation</h1>
<p>
  These instructions will guide you through the DevZero CLI installation process based on your local
  operating system. Our CLI enables you to build, launch, and connect to your team's workspaces.
</p>
<div>
  <p>
    <div>MacOS and/or Linux Installation</div>
  </p>
  <p>Run the following command from within your terminal: </p>
  <p>
    <div>
      <div>
        <div>curl -fsSL https://get.devzero.io | sh</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </p>
</div>
<div>
  <p>
    <div>Windows Installations</div>
  </p>
  <p>
    Our Windows CLI requires{' '}
    <a href="https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/wsl/install">WSL 2</a>. We do not support WSL
    1. To verify your WSL version you can run the following from your Command shell or
    PowerShell:{' '}
  </p>
  <pre>
    <code class="language-sh">wsl -l -v</code>
  </pre>
  <p>
    If you need to install or upgrade your WSL version please{' '}
    <a href="https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/wsl/install#upgrade-version-from-wsl-1-to-wsl-2">
      follow these instructions
    </a>
    . <br />
    <br />
    Once you have confirmed your WSL version, please open the Linux shell and run the following
    command:{' '}
  </p>
  <pre>
    <code class="language-bash">curl -fsSL https://get.devzero.io | sh</code>
  </pre>
</div>
